Limitless paper in a paperless world

Dunder Mifflin, Inc. is a mid-cap regional paper and office supply distributor with an emphasis on servicing small-business clients. Founded by Robert Dunder and Robert Mifflin in 1949, the company was originally an industrial supplier of metal brackets, mostly for construction. And then Mifflin, of course he killed himself later. Dunder met Mifflin through the Rotary Club. Mifflin was at dinner with Beverly and her husband, Jerry Trupiano from South Jersey and he was tall. Both he and Mifflin were tall guys.
